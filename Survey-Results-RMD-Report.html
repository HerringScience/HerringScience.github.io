HSC Survey Results

Scots Bay Survey # 6 Results from 07/08/2022

Survey Details

8 commercial purse seiners conducted this fishing survey on 07/08/2022 starting at 21:00:00.

6 vessels ran two transects each in the Main Survey Box, the Morning Star ran four lines in the Eastern Survey Box, and the Brunswick Provider ran four lines in the Northern Survey Box.

There was a plankton tow and replicate conducted and CTD cast by Lady Janice II.

A total of 2,593 tags were applied during the survey over 13 events from 2022-08-03 to 2022-08-11, from 5 vessels.

Samples were obtained for target strength estimation.

Tides Tables

Tide Schedule for Margaretsville NS, Station #315, Source: www.waterlevels.gc.ca

Acoustic Equipment

Each vessel was equipped with a Simrad ES38-18/200-18C combi (2-in-1) transducer prior to the survey that contains a 38kHz split beam and 200 kHz single beam transducer. Data was logged to the computer hard-drive for post-processing. All participating boats were calibrated on 38kHz before the survey.

Survey Results

Main Results

The results are subject to change as DFO will re-evaluate data editing, area estimation and apply a target strength.

Preliminary biomass prediction for Scots Bay using standardized parallel transects is 5,250mt, and with turnover 3,067mt.
